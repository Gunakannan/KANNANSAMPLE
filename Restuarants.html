SALEM RR BIRIYANI:

Salem RR Biryani is a popular biryani restaurant located in major cities in Tamil Nadu. The restaurant is known for serving authentic biryani made with traditional South Indian spices and ingredients. Salem RR Biryani is typically prepared with fragrant Basmati rice, marinated chicken or mutton, and a blend of aromatic spices such as cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon. The dish is cooked in a traditional dum-style, where the biryani is sealed in a pot with a lid and cooked over low heat for several hours, allowing the flavors to infuse and the meat to become tender and juicy. Apart from biryani, Salem RR Biryani also offers a variety of other South Indian delicacies such as Chicken 65, Chettinadu Pepper Chicken, and Mutton Keema etc..
